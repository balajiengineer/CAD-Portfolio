# 📌 Project Overview

The RFID Hybrid Enclosure was designed as a robust industrial housing for an RFID-based electronic system. The design integrates plastic, aluminium and composite components into a compact enclosure while ensuring structural integrity, manufacturability and ease of assembly.

The project covered the complete mechanical design process from concept development through production-ready CAD models, engineering drawings and manufacturing documentation.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Develop a compact RFID enclosure
- Improve structural rigidity
- Ensure manufacturability
- Simplify assembly
- Protect internal electronic components
- Reduce manufacturing complexity

# 🏭 Manufacturing Processes

The enclosure was designed considering multiple manufacturing processes:

- Plastic Injection Moulding
- CNC Machining
- CNC Routing
- Waterjet Cutting
- Sheet Metal Bending

---

# ⚙ Key Design Considerations

- Uniform Wall Thickness
- 2° Draft Angle
- Minimum Bend Radius
- Standard Fasteners (SS304)
- Manufacturing Tolerances (±0.2 mm)
- Deburring & Surface Finish
- Assembly Accessibility
